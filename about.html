About Us

       Founded in 2019, PETSY Hotel was created with a simple yet important mission – to provide a welcoming and comfortable place for both guests and their beloved pets. As passionate pet owners ourselves, we understand the joy and companionship that pets bring, which is why we've designed every aspect of our hotel to cater to the needs of pets and their owners. From spacious, pet-friendly rooms to dedicated play areas and pampering spa services, we strive to make every stay memorable and enjoyable for you and your furry friend. At PETSY Hotel, your pet is more than just a guest—they're family.

Mr. Charles Babbage

Founder & CEO of PETSY

       "Mr. Charles Babbage - Founder of PETSY Pet-Friendly Hotel* Mr. Charles Babbage is the founder of PETSY, a pet-friendly hotel designed to provide a comfortable and welcoming space for both pets and their owners. With a passion for animals and a background in hospitality, Charles created PETSY to fill a gap in the market for accommodations where pets are treated like family. His love for pets, combined with his experience in the hotel industry, led him to create a place where guests can relax and enjoy their stay alongside their furry companions. Under his leadership, PETSY has become known for its pet-friendly amenities, spacious play areas, and luxurious rooms. Charles is also a strong advocate for animal welfare, supporting pet adoption and other initiatives to improve the lives of animals. His dedication to both pets and their owners has made PETSY a beloved destination for all."
